Welcome to Phytolens

At Phytolens, we are revolutionizing agriculture through AI-powered plant disease detection. Our mission is to empower farmers and researchers with cutting-edge machine learning tools that enhance crop health and productivity.

Rani Soni, the visionary behind Phytolens, is a pre-final year undergraduate majoring in Computer Science and Engineering at Rajiv Gandhi Proudyogiki Vishwavidyalaya, Bhopal. With a strong foundation in machine learning and data science, Rani has completed over 20 projects, participated in leading hackathons, and contributed to various community initiatives.

Rani has earned more than 100 badges and 30+ trophies from various learning platforms, showcasing a deep passion for learning and growth. They've interned at top companies such as Cisco and Accenture and participated in AI/ML-focused programs by Google Developer Student Clubs.

Their proficiency in data structures, algorithms, and problem-solving is complemented by a love for applying machine learning to real-world challenges.
